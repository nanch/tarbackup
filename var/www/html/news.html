2012-10-07

Backup and restore tar files of up to 100MB from the command-line with curl!
# curl https://tarbackup.com/api/v1/backup.php -u account:password -F "file=@backup.tar.gz.enc"

# curl https://tarbackup.com/api/v1/restore.php?file=backup.tar.gz.enc -u account:password > backup.tar.gz.enc

A lot of feedback regarding tarbackup has been that the service should not be free. The reasoning behind this is that a free service is not sustainable in the long run, which is true. So today I'm announcing tar backup service pricing at $0.07/GB/mo. This will allow me to pay for expenses and provide a reliable service into the future.
